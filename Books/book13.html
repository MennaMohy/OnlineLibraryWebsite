The Boy from the Woods

- Harlan Coben

    Wilde was found living wild in the woods as a child with no memory of his life. Now an adult with military skills, he's drawn into danger when a teenage girl vanishes from their wealthy suburban community. As Wilde investigates, he uncovers a web of lies connecting a reality TV star, a ruthless lawyer, and shocking truths about his own mysterious past.
