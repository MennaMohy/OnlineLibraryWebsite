Things We Never Got Over

- Lucy Score

    When Naomi flees her wedding to rescue her estranged twin, she lands in Knockemout, Virginia—and crashes straight into Knox, the brooding barber who hates drama. But with a niece to protect and a past that won’t let go, Naomi might just be the chaos he never saw coming.
