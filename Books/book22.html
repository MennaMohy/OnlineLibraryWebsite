The Enemy

- Sarah Adams

    Ambitious event planner Evie and laid-back musician Ryan can’t stand each other—until a forced work collaboration leads to something else. Now, with their careers on the line and tension at an all-time high, they’ll have to decide: Is this just a temporary truce, or are they playing for keeps?
