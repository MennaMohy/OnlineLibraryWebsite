Love Game

- Emma Rae

    Elle’s love language is food, but the culinary world hasn’t been kind—until she lands her dream role as personal chef to tennis superstar Nicky Salco. Handsome, disciplined, and totally off-limits, Nicky is everything she shouldn’t want. But when late-night hunger strikes during his grueling Wimbledon training, their kitchen rendezvous turn into something far more tempting.
