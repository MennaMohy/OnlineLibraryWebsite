Verity

- Colleen Hoover

    Struggling writer Lowen Ashleigh accepts a dream job: completing bestselling author Verity Crawford's popular book series after a tragic accident leaves her unable to write. But when Lowen discovers Verity's unpublished autobiography—filled with bone-chilling confessions—the line between fact and fiction blurs.
